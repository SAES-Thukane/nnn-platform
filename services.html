What We Do

Services

NNN operates at the intersection of creative production and cultural curation. We work with brands, artists, and institutions who understand that the underground is where culture is actually written.

Offerings

01

Event Production

End-to-end conceptualisation, production, and execution of curated cultural events — from intimate 100-person gatherings to large-scale activations.

  • — Venue sourcing & negotiation
  • — Talent booking & artist liaison
  • — Ticketing & access management
  • — On-site production & logistics

02

Creative Direction

Visual identity, art direction, and aesthetic consulting for brands looking to embed themselves authentically within youth and subculture spaces.

  • — Brand identity & visual language
  • — Campaign art direction
  • — Content strategy & production
  • — Photography & documentation briefs

03

Brand Partnerships

Strategic integration of brand partners into NNN's existing ecosystem. We only take on partners whose values align with our community's.

  • — Event co-branding & sponsorship
  • — Product seeding & activations
  • — Community co-creation campaigns
  • — Long-term partnership structuring

04

Cultural Consulting

Advisory services for brands and institutions looking to understand and engage Joburg's underground scene without extracting from it.

  • — Market & community insight
  • — Youth culture trend mapping
  • — Authentic narrative development
  • — Community liaison & outreach
